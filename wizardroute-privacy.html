Wizard Route — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Wizard Route is a single-player mobile game. We have designed it to require no account, no online connection, and no data collection of any kind.

What we collect

Nothing. Wizard Route does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, device identifiers, location, contacts, photos, or usage analytics. The app does not make network requests.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your local settings (music on/off, vibration on/off, practice mode, level length, music track selection) using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism. This information stays on your device, is not transmitted anywhere, and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Third-party services

Wizard Route does not use any third-party advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or social SDKs. There are no in-app purchases.

Children's privacy

Wizard Route does not knowingly collect any information from children, or from anyone else. Since the game collects no information at all, it is safe for use by players of any age in this respect.

Changes to this policy

If our practices ever change — for example, if we add an optional online feature in a future update — we will update this page and the date above before the change takes effect.
