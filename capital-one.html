Context

I joined as a Principal Associate Cybersecurity Engineer to lead the IAM program at scale, building on deep SailPoint and Okta expertise developed across prior engagements. The role combines hands-on delivery of integration work with the platform-level patterns and reusable components used across engineering teams in a multi-year migration program.
