Context

The Vanguard Group is one of the world’s largest asset managers, with strict regulatory and compliance requirements across SOX, internal audit, and access governance. The IAM program is mission-critical — it touches every employee, contractor, and service account inside the firm.

I served as the Application Engineering Technical Lead on the IAM program. This is where I built deep SailPoint expertise — leading SailPoint IIQ development, designing certification campaigns for compliance, integrating governance into SaaS like Google Workspace, and contributing to the firm’s SailPoint ISC migration.

  • Contributed to the firm’s SailPoint ISC migration, developing reusable components to accelerate delivery.
  • Acted as primary technical interface between engineering, security, and business stakeholders — scoping, estimating, and presenting program progress to executive sponsors.
