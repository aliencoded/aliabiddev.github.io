Wizard Route

A magical squad runner for iOS. Lead a coven of wizards through enchanted ruins, dodge red holes in the floor, and survive the boss arena waiting at the end of each route.

App Store — coming soon Support Privacy Policy

What it is

You steer a coven of wizards down a procedurally-built path through four worlds — Forest, Desert, Underground, and Volcano. Your wizards auto-fire at enemies; your job is to dodge, break through gates, free new mage classes from statues, and stay alive long enough to clear the boss waiting at the end of each route.

Highlights

  • Four mage classesBolt, Spread, Hex, and Pyromancer — each with a different projectile pattern.
  • Themed routesForest, Desert, Underground, Volcano — with random night-time variants.
  • Boss arenasOrbit around the boss while dodging slams, volleys, charges, and homing missiles.
  • Endless modeClear all four routes and the run keeps going with escalating difficulty.
  • No ads, no IAPSingle download. No tracking, no data collection.
  • Practice modeLearn boss patterns without taking damage.

Status

The first build has been uploaded to App Store Connect and is currently in Apple’s review queue. Once approved, this page will link directly to the App Store listing.
